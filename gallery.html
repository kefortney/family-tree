The Fortun Church & Fantofte Stavkirke

The original Fortun Stave Church stood in the village until 1883, when the congregation sold it to Bergen merchants. It became the famous Fantofte Stavkirke — and was burned by arsonists in 1992. It was rebuilt and stands today in Bergen as a major cultural landmark.
